I am interested in the theory (and algorithms) of modern machine learning.

Currently, I am a Ph.D. student at Stanford. I am extremely fortunate to be advised by Prof. Andrea Montanari.
I also work closely with Prof. Jason D. Lee.

I received my B.S. in Mathematics from Peking University in 2024. Previously, I was fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jason D. Lee and Prof. Lei Wu for my undergraduate research.

I am also open to discussion for other interesting topics. If you are interested in connecting with me, please feel free to reach out.
